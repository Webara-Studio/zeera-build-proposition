04 / RESPONSIBILITY SPLIT
Who builds what

Webara builds the layer
Zeera owns the relationship.

A disciplined split avoids pretending one supplier can do everything — and gives Zeera control of the parts that create local advantage.

WebaraFront end, wallet experience, local language, social sharing, referral programme and local configuration.
PartnerOdds, orderbook/liquidity, managed trading, risk, sports data and settlement services.
PSP / KYCPayment rails, identity verification, AML screening and regulated service components.
ZeeraBrand, licence, customer relationship, commercial decisions, support and operating governance.

The strategic advantage

Zeera is not locked into a supplier’s generic user interface. Webara creates an integration layer that allows Zeera to improve, localise and change components over time.

08 / BUILD ROADMAP
Build before scale

A phased route from
integration to traction.

The first milestone is not a large feature list. It is a dependable, testable operating core.

PHASE 01

Partner & compliance design

Confirm Ghana operating model, sportsbook partner, PSP routes, KYC/AML providers, data flows, responsibilities and commercial terms.

PHASE 02

Core build

Build front end, wallet states, authentication, connector layer, language framework, referral engine and operations console.

PHASE 03

Sandbox & pilot

Test deposits, withdrawals, KYC, odds, settlement, failures, limits, referrals and support workflows before controlled launch.

PHASE 04

Market activation

Launch with a focused sports catalogue, creator/referral partnerships and measured customer acquisition. Improve from real usage.

Build the rails first. Then turn on the growth.

Payments

Age, KYC, AML, responsible gaming, audit access and regulator cooperation.

Integration

API, sandbox, webhooks, uptime, latency, incident response and data export.

Commercials

Setup, minimums, revenue share, reserves, term, exit and all hidden charges.
